Through DUWE, consumers who love to “Shop Black” can actively participate in group economics with an infrastructure that financially aids in the development of our communities. Conversely, Black Business owners can expand their customer reach to increase profits and obtain business resources. “Black people must practice group economics to survive in this capitalistic society.” ~Claude Anderson
